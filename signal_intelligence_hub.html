Standard Brief Architecture

Signal
The raw news event. 1–2 sentences only. No interpretation. Just what happened.
02

Commercial implications. What changes because of this. Why it matters now.

Client-specific impact. Their strategy, assets, and initiatives made explicit.

07
Big Bet

08
Insight Statement
Exactly one sentence. Signal meets client strategy. The brief's editorial conclusion.
